# Project: Saath

## Vision

Saath is a hyperlocal activity network that helps people find other people nearby to do things with.

The goal is not to create another social media platform.

The goal is to help users move from:

Idea → Conversation → Plan → Real Life Activity

---

# Core Mission

> Find people nearby to do things with.

Examples:

- Find badminton players
- Find movie buddies
- Find jogging partners
- Find food explorers
- Find gaming teammates
- Find local communities

---

# Problem We Are Solving

Today people usually:

- Ask existing friends
- Ask WhatsApp groups
- Give up on the activity

Saath helps users discover people outside their existing circle who share the same interests and are nearby.

---

# Target Audience

Primary:

- 16-35 years old
- Students
- Young professionals
- Sports enthusiasts
- People new to the city
- People looking to expand their circle

Initial Market:

- Ulhasnagar
- Kalyan
- Dombivli

---

# Product Philosophy

We are NOT building:

- Instagram
- Facebook
- Reddit
- Discord

We ARE building:

> A Local Action Network

Every feature should answer:

"Will this help users meet, connect, or do something together?"

If not, it should not exist.

# Navigation Structure

## Home (Activities)

Main feed of activity conversations.

Examples:

🏸 Need 2 badminton players

🎬 Movie this Saturday

🏃 Morning jogging group

🍜 Trying a new cafe

Users can:

- Create conversation
- Join conversation
- Chat
- Add location
- Plan activities

---

## Explore

Discovery page.

Shows:

- Trending conversations
- Popular categories
- Active communities
- Nearby opportunities

Purpose:

Help users discover things happening around them.

NOT:

- Memes
- Celebrity news
- Random content

---

## Business

Local businesses can create profiles.

Examples:

- Sports arenas
- Gyms
- Cafes
- Restaurants
- Coaches
- Event organizers

Business post types:

- Offer
- Event
- Service
- Announcement

Purpose:

Help users discover places and opportunities.

---

# Conversation Lifecycle

## Planning

People discussing.

No venue/time decided.

Example:

"Anyone up for badminton this weekend?"

---

## Confirmed

Venue and time decided.

Example:

Saturday 6 PM
@Weekly Sports Arena

---

## Archived

Conversation automatically closes after activity is completed or inactive.
