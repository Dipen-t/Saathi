# Product

## Register

product

## Users
16-35 years old (Students, Young professionals, Sports enthusiasts, People new to the city, People looking to expand their circle). They are looking to find people nearby to do things with (sports, movies, food, fitness).

## Product Purpose
Saath is a hyperlocal activity network. It helps users move from: Idea → Conversation → Plan → Real Life Activity. Every feature must help users meet, connect, or do something together locally.

## Brand Personality
Friendly, energetic, local, youthful, clean, and trustworthy. It should feel like a community app combined with a modern messenger.

## Anti-references
- Corporate software
- Over-designed startup landing pages
- Traditional social media feeds (Instagram, Facebook, Reddit, Discord)

## Design Principles
- **Action over content**: Focus on real-life activities, not infinite scrolling or content consumption.
- **Local context first**: Emphasize proximity and immediacy ("Something is happening nearby").
- **Fast and clean**: Modern, minimal interface that gets out of the way.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Large touch targets and mobile-first readability.
